# PAGE TEMPLATES — section-by-section blueprint for every page type

Each template lists its **purpose**, **primary conversion**, **section stack** (in order), the **objection/question each section answers**, **schema**, and whether it has a **PPC variant**. Sections marked ✦ are shared, componentised building blocks reused across templates (and reusable for Google Ads). *(Pending Client Confirmation)* marks copy that needs client facts.

**Shared components (build once, reuse everywhere ✦):** Sticky Header (logo, nav, Call+WhatsApp) · Hero (variant per template) · Trust Bar · Offer Card · Benefit Grid · Objection Block · Proof/Testimonial Block · Coverage Map · How-It-Works Steps · Product Card · FAQ (FAQPage schema) · CTA Band · Cross-sell Teaser · Floating WhatsApp · Sticky Mobile CTA Bar · Footer (full nav + both NAPs) · Breadcrumbs.

## T5a — `/used-cooking-oil-collection/get-paid/`
Lead with pricing transparency ("what your used oil is worth per litre" / live quote), free drums, free collection, qualified by volume. Targets *sell/who buys used cooking oil, price per litre*. CTA "Get Paid for Your Used Oil". Keep the rand figure current or framed as a quote. PPC variant: **yes** (cash-intent campaigns convert hard).

## T5b — `/used-cooking-oil-collection/compliance/`
Lead with the safe-disposal certificate + by-law explainer + licensed status ("your protection at inspection"). Targets *used cooking oil disposal, compliant UCO*. Deep link to Cluster-2 compliance articles. PPC variant: yes (inspection-driven).

## T5c — `/grease-trap-cleaning/`
Own indexable page for the existing service (competitors bundle it). Targets *grease trap cleaning [city]*. Cross-sell UCO collection. PPC variant: optional.

## T5d — `/uco-compliance-reporting/`
Upgrade of the current form-less `/uco-report`. For franchises/groups: store-level reporting, cost/quality/compliance/ESG benefits. **Add a real qualifying form.** Targets *UCO reporting, franchise oil compliance*. Highest-value segment. PPC variant: yes (franchise/group).

## T5e — `/cooking-oil-recycling/`
Biodiesel / circular-economy / ESG story. Targets *cooking oil recycling South Africa*. Feeds E-E-A-T + sustainability positioning; links to the "how UCO becomes biodiesel" article. PPC variant: no (informational-commercial).
