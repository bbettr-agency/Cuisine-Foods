## Collection economics — collectors PAY for used oil
- SA UCO collectors BUY used cooking oil from kitchens: ~**R3–R8 per litre**, varying by region, volume and quality. `[verified]`
  - Durban area: R3.00–R5.00/litre. Another buyer: R8.00/litre. Gauteng & North West: ~R4.00/litre. `[verified — WebSearch, multiple SA collectors]`
- Payment models: Cash-on-Delivery (after invoice) or VGM (weigh-then-pay after loading). `[verified]`
- Implication: for restaurants, UCO is a **revenue stream + free removal**, not a cost. Key persuasion hook. `[verified]`

## Regulation & compliance (SA)
- **Waste transfer / safe-disposal certificate:** waste oil, grease & fats should be collected by a **licensed waste-oil collector** at regular intervals; a copy of the **waste transfer certificate must be kept ≥2 years** and made available for inspection per wastewater by-laws. `[verified]`
- **Municipal FOG by-laws (Cape Town, Johannesburg):** strict limits on fats/oils/grease entering sewers; **grease traps mandatory** for food businesses; illegal dumping → substantial fines/penalties, possible closure of kitchen. Grease traps recommended cleaned every ~30 days. `[verified]`
- **ROSE Foundation** (est. 1994): voluntary extended-producer-responsibility scheme; accredited oil collectors collect used oil and **issue generators with safe-disposal certificates as required by the Waste Act**. `[verified]`
- **NORA-SA** (National Oil Recycling Association of SA): industry body; members manage safe, compliant collection/transport/recycling of used oil (e.g. EnviroServ is a member). `[verified]`
- Food-safety risk: repeated reuse/degradation of oil produces toxic compounds (acrylamide — WHO carcinogen; PAHs). Illegal re-selling of UCO back into food chain is a known global food-fraud risk; strong reason for compliant, documented disposal. `[verified — general; SA-specific incident coverage thin]`

## Recycling destinations
- Collected UCO → **biodiesel** feedstock (local + potential EU/renewable-diesel/SAF export), **oleochemicals**, animal feed. Sustainability/ESG framing. `[verified]`
- ISCC certification appears among leading SA recyclers (e.g. Scope Oils "ISCC Certified"). `[verified]`
