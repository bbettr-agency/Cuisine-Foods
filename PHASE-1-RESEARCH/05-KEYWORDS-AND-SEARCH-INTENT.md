## Key SA search reality
- **UCO is a crowded, active, high-intent market** — dedicated competitors + Facebook groups + Gumtree rank for "who buys used cooking oil", "sell used cooking oil", collection + price queries. `[SERP-observed]`
- **UCO pricing is public and low** — R5/L, R5–R7/L, even R2.50/L appear in snippets → price-sensitive, cash-incentive framing works. `[SERP-observed]`
- **Bulk supply SERPs** are dominated by manufacturers + wholesale marketplaces (Makro/PriceCheck on product-price queries), not directories → winnable with real supplier pages. `[SERP-observed]`
- **20L is the canonical SA pack** — palm olein 20L ≈ R569–R680, sunflower ≈ R570–R640 in results; buyers search product + "20L" + price. `[SERP-observed]`
- **Western Cape UCO is a genuine gap** — fewer, regional players; one Gauteng collector even excludes WC. `[SERP-observed]`
- **Cuisine Foods already ranks** for several UCO + supply terms → domain has baseline authority to build on. `[SERP-observed]`
