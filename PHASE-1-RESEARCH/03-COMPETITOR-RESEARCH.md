## The 5 biggest opportunities to outperform
1. **Location-page gap (biggest/easiest):** almost nobody targets cities (only Rein Oil, thinly). Build genuine, unique province + metro pages for both supply and UCO.
2. **Trust arsenal gap:** everyone is weak (fake testimonials / single cert / "awaiting ISO"). Win with real proof — years, licensed/certified status, safe-disposal certificate, client types, fleet, Google reviews, B-BBEE.
3. **Content-depth gap:** only Coastal publishes real education, and it isn't geo-targeted. Match its depth + localise + own the UCO compliance angle no Gauteng player touches.
4. **Pricing/"cash for oil" transparency gap:** nobody shows UCO pricing; AJ shouts "cash" with no numbers. A transparent rebate/how-pricing-works page out-converts the field.
5. **Conversion-UX gap:** most rely on a bare phone/"Contact Us." Double down on WhatsApp + click-to-call + quote form on every page; capture the dead scopeoils.com rankings.

**Winning patterns to adopt:** ISCC/compliance credentials + certificate (UCO), "cash for your used oil" hook, biodiesel/environmental education, WhatsApp conversion, product clarity in 20L, "supplier to restaurants/takeaways" language.
